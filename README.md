Dot Calendar Wallpaper

A minimalist Android live wallpaper inspired by the viral 2026 Instagram productivity trend that visualizes the entire year using 365 dots. Each dot represents one day, gradually filling up as the year progresses, helping users stay mindful of time and maintain daily consistency.

The concept is simple: 365 days → 365 dots → 1 productive year.

✨ Features

📆 365-Day Visualization – Each dot represents one day of the year

🎯 Minimalist Productivity Tool – No distractions, just visual progress

📈 Daily Motivation – See how much of the year has passed

📱 Live Wallpaper – Works directly on your Android home screen

🎨 Clean & Aesthetic Design – Inspired by modern productivity trends

💡 Idea Behind the Project

Many productivity apps require constant interaction and are often abandoned after a few days.

A wallpaper, however, is something you see every time you unlock your phone.

By converting the year into a grid of 365 dots, this wallpaper provides a constant visual reminder that each day matters.

Watching the dots fill over time encourages:

Consistency

Discipline

Mindful time usage

Progress tracking
